Who We Are

Pioneering Renewable Energy in India

Shree Shyam Eco Systems Private Limited is a diversified renewable energy company founded with a vision to contribute to India's clean energy transition. We operate across multiple verticals in the renewable energy sector, focusing on hydropower generation and biodiesel production.

Our mission is to deliver sustainable energy solutions that benefit both the environment and the communities we serve. With strategic investments in proven technologies and a commitment to excellence, we are building a cleaner, greener future for generations to come.
