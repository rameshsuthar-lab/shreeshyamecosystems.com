Production Process

From Waste to Fuel

1. Raw Oil Collection

Collection of used cooking oil and non-edible oils from various sources

2. Filtration & Pretreatment

Cleaning and removing impurities from raw materials

3. Transesterification

Chemical process to convert oil to biodiesel

4. Separation & Purification

Separating biodiesel from glycerin

5. Final Output

Quality-tested biodiesel ready for use
